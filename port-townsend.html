Best Bookstore

William James Bookstore is a charming independent shop specializing in used and rare titles. Located in the heart of downtown on Water Street, it is easy to spend hours browsing their collection.

Best Pub

The Pour House offers a rotating selection of absolutely exquisite craft beer. If you want a 12% stout or a Hazy IPA with orange juice, this is the spot for you. Outside, the patio extends all the way to the beach. Perfect for summer days.

Local Beer

Hazy IPAs are particularly popular in this region, with several microbreweries focusing on juicy, aromatic hops. For an ultra-local option, check out Port Townsend Brewery's Hop Diggity beer.
